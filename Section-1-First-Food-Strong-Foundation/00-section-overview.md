# Section 1 — First Food: Strong Foundation (Overview)

*Source videos: "What's in it — First Food, Strong Foundation" and "Q&A — Let's be clear about the beginning"*

## What this section covers
Section 1 is the foundation of the course. It answers the core starting questions every parent has when beginning solid food:

- **When exactly should solid food start, and what signs confirm the baby is ready?**
- **What should the baby's very first meal/food be?** (Includes the Annaprashan kheer recipe — given inside the "first food" lesson at **[00:00:34]**, where the instructor points you to watch that video.)
- **What to do if the baby eats nothing, or throws and plays with food.**
- **How many times a day to feed, and how to plan and time meals.**
- **What to cook and what the right food combination is.**
- How to begin a meal and recognise when it is "set."

The instructor stresses going **step by step** — finish Section 1 before moving to Section 2.

## Q&A video note
The Section 1 Q&A video has almost no narration. At **[00:00:29]** the instructor asks viewers to *"come to the screen for 5 minutes and read all these slides."* **The actual Q&A questions and answers are shown on-screen as slides in the video, not spoken** — to capture them, the on-screen slides must be read directly from the video around the **00:00:29** mark onward.

## The goal of the section
A **planned, peaceful, confident parent** who is sure that what they give the child is right for them.
