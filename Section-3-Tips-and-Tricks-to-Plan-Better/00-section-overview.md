# Section 3 — Tips & Tricks to Be a Smart Mumma & Plan Better (Overview)

*Source videos: "What's in it — Tips & tricks to make you a smart mumma" and "Q&A — Questions you might have due to family pressure"*

## What this section covers
This section tackles the **family-pressure / "we did it differently in our day"** topics and planning questions:
- **Salt and sugar** — "we gave it to our kids, what's your problem?"
- The key framing: *it's not that the old ways were wrong — there was simply no research then; today there is research, so we follow it.* Understand the research base so you can handle these conversations.
- **Shopping list** — what exactly to buy.
- **Balancing feed and food** — the child asks for milk even an hour after a full meal; how to know if the stomach is full.
- Plus: eating without teeth, weight-gain foods, recipes, ghee/oil, age-wise guide, seasonal changes, and a guide to the MyDvija product range.
