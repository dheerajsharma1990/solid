# 3.30 — Detailed Guide to Using the MyDvija Homemade Baby-Food Range

*An on-request product video. Each product is **shown on screen** as it's described; all are made homestyle in the MyDvija kitchen, the same way you'd cook for your own child. Refer to the video for the visuals.*

1. **Barley / sattu flour** — traditional grain (said to date to Mahabharata times). Good source of **protein, fiber, energy**. Make thin kheer/porridge for the baby, or roti/bhakri/paratha. Barley **water** works as a weaning food and helps **control blood sugar**; barley flour bhakri also aids **weight loss** for mothers. **All age groups.**
2. **Oats flour** — **never give oats raw**; must be **processed** (roast then powder, or soak in milk/water). Make dosa, chilla, idli, roti, pancake, waffle, laddu; can mix with other flours. Good **fiber**, prevents constipation. **All age groups.**
3. **Khichdi mix** — moong dal + rice + jeera powder (washed, soaked overnight, roasted, powdered). **After 8 months** as **instant food**: add to water, fry in ghee (no lumps), or cook with grated carrot/veg + 2 drops lemon. Great when travelling — add boiling water, then more water, cover, ready.
4. **Natural noodles (3 flavors)** — **ragi/nachni noodles, multi-millet noodles** (kodo, proso, bajra, ragi), **moringa noodles** (drumstick-leaf powder). Ragi grown on **3-years-fertilizer-free** farms; naturally made (not machine-dried); spice packet has **no Ajinomoto/soda**. **From 6 months** (no spice mix for very small babies; spice mix okay ~8 months). Binding agent = ragi/wheat flour. No refined flour → takes longer to cook (~5–15 min).
5. **Ragi pasta & multi-millet pasta** — good **finger food**, **from 6 months** (no spice mix; spice mix ~8 months). Boil water + 2 drops ghee/oil, add ~20–25 pieces, cook 5–10 min. Homemade sauce = roasted gram flour (or wheat flour) + curd + water; or the instructor's version: ghee + ginger-garlic + sauteed chopped veg + roasted wheat flour + water + pepper (+ salt and milk for older kids).
6. **Nachni sattva** (best-seller) — sprouted, sun-dried, roasted, powdered ragi → easy digestion. Make sweet/non-sweet porridge, laddu, dosa, pancake, waffle, bhakri, thepla.
7. **Wheat sattva** — same process with wheat; same uses.
   - *Pancake/chilla how-to:* any flour + grated veg (savory: add chopped coriander) → idli-batter consistency; sweet: add grated apple + jaggery.
8. **MyDvija Jaggery** — **iron-rich** (made in iron vessels; market jaggery often isn't) from **chemical-free, 3-years-fertilizer-free** sugarcane. Use to add iron + sweetness.
9. **Nutri Dalia** — broken wheat/brown rice/bajra + sesame, carom seeds, dal, whole grains → a **cereal + protein** combo. Temper with ginger-garlic + cumin + grated veg, roast, add dal, water, 5–7 whistles, finish with ghee (+ salt if 1 year+).
10. **Cerelac & Satvic Mix (instant, for travel)** — Satvic Mix = cardamom flavor; Cerelac Mix = dal + whole grain + rice + makhana + almond; strong cereal-protein combo (no fruit-pulp/flavor claims). Add boiling water, mix (no lumps), more water, cover **10 min**.
11. **Multigrain flour** — popular; whole, rich **protein + cereal** source; make waffle, laddu, chilla, pancake, idli, paratha, dosa, roti.
12. **Flaxseed powder** — **superfood** (omega-3, brain food). Add a pinch to any **savory** baby food; pairs with fresh drumstick/moringa leaves.
13. **Moringa powder** — excellent **B12 and iron**; a **must-have for all mothers** to replenish **post-delivery** loss.
