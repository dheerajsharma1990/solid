# 3.25 — 60+ Recipe Ideas (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks, Soups)

~10 ideas per meal across 6 slots (3 meals + 3 snacks) ≈ **60+ recipes**. (More in the *Baby's Yum Food Diary* — 300+ recipes; new recipes on the **MyDvija YouTube channel every Monday & Thursday**.)

## Breakfast
- **Chilla** of any flour (wheat sattva, nachni sattva, multigrain mix, khichdi mix, etc.). Soak flour in **curd**, add **rice flour** for crispiness + any **grated vegetable**; roll and serve.
- **Waffles** (small waffle machine from Amazon): wheat-flour batter + a little baking soda + **jaggery**.
- **Idli, uttapam** (small button idlis).
- **Pancakes**: mango, banana, apple, walnut, dry-fruit.

## 11 am snack
- For weight gain: **banana, sweet potato, avocado**.
- For daycare: **toast-paneer, Rajgira/amaranth chikki, amaranth laddu, homemade ragi laddu**.

## Lunch (use the "family-pot" plate so it mirrors your plate)
- **Cutlets**: e.g., **rajma-beetroot-potato cutlet** plated with a little crushed rajma, finely chopped tomato, grated beetroot.
- **Parathas/theplas**: paneer paratha, aloo paratha, methi thepla, Maharashtrian **thalipeeth**.
- **Sabzi-roti, pulao, kadhi-khichdi, proper khichdi.** Don't repeat the same item daily.

## 4 pm snack
- Breastfed babies often just breastfeed; else: **churmura, makhana, lahi (puffed grain), cheese cube/slice, sauteed paneer, sauteed tofu**.

## 6 pm snack — SOUPS (10 ideas)
1. **Malagatani (mulligatawny) soup** — a little lentils + moong dal + any veggies, cooked in cooker, hand-mixed, pepper + black salt.
2. **Bottle-gourd soup** — bottle gourd + capsicum + ½ onion + ginger, cooked & blended, pepper + black salt or jeera powder (add salt only if 1 year+).
3. **Red-pumpkin soup** — red pumpkin + carrot + 1 spinach leaf, with ghee + garlic tempering.
4. **Onion soup** — saute onion in ghee, add rice flour, saute, cook in water, blend, pepper + pink salt.
5. **Broccoli (almond-broccoli) soup** — ghee + almonds + broccoli, saute with garlic & onion, cook, salt + pepper.
6. **Cream of zucchini** — oil + ghee + zucchini + rice flour + garlic, cook in water, blend, pepper + pink salt (avoid salt for very small babies).
7. **Tomato soup** — cook tomato, smash well, strain.
8. **Tulsi soup** — tulsi + mint + coriander + curry leaves boiled in water, blended, with cumin + a pinch of turmeric; serve warm — good for the throat.
9. **Mix-veg soup** — french beans, peas, potato, carrot, red pumpkin (any), cooked & blended, with pepper/cumin/mint/curry-leaf/garlic/ginger tempering; for 1 year+ add 2 drops of milk for color.

## Dinner (make it a little "fancy")
- **Pasta / noodles** (multi-millet pasta, ragi/nachni noodles — from MyDvija), **idli, uttapam, tikkis** (e.g., dal-chawal tikki), **cutlet with french fries**, homemade **pav bhaji**.
- *"Khichdi morning, noon, and night is not a menu"* — vary it.

## Useful additions & binders
- **Protein adds:** soy chunks, tofu, paneer, soy keema, cashew (kaju) gravy.
- **Binders:** poha, rava (rolled on top), potato, beetroot, sweet potato.
- **Non-veg cutlets** also fine.
