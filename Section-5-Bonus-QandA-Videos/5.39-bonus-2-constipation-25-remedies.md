# 5.39 — Bonus 2: Constipation — 25 Home Remedies After Food Introduction

Foods/combinations with good **fiber + hydration** to relieve (or prevent) constipation. *(Re-confirms the emergency signs from Bonus 1: blood in potty / very hard tummy / bleeding tear / severely red anus → doctor, not home remedies. Any **artificial/medicinal laxative** needs the doctor first.)*

1. **Baby tummy massage** in a specific way (daily, especially during food introduction).
2. **Calm parent** — panic creates psychological stress for the baby too.
3. **Warm-water bath** (with a bedtime massage) — relaxes muscles → easier morning potty.
4. **Introduce water** sip by sip after 6 months (~8–10 drops/sips across the day; the baby doesn't need a lot).
5. **More breastfeeding** — breastmilk is ~80% water, prevents dehydration → smoother potty.
6. **Prune juice** (prunes = "potato fever"/dried plums) — one of the best remedies. Soak overnight, make a paste/juice, give **10–20 ml**.
7. **Apple + carrot juice/smoothie** — steam apple (with peel) + carrot together (little water), puree.
8. **Spinach smoothie/soup** (or add spinach to khichdi/dal) — boosts fiber.
9. **Sweet potato** (boiled/mashed, smoothie, or soup) — its fiber helps stool come smoothly (it does **not** worsen pain).
10. **Oats** (porridge, smoothie, dosa, pancake) — fiber smoothens potty.
11. **Dates** — add dates paste (very beneficial).
12. **Raisins (brown) water/juice** — soak 7–8 overnight, blend (keep the fiber, no need to strain), give **10–20 ml**.
13. **Orange + carrot smoothie** — vitamin C + vitamin A → immunity + smooth bowel movement.
14. **Barley** — barley water, or 1.5 tsp soaked barley cooked into the dal-rice khichdi; or barley soup.
15. **Broccoli** — vitamin C + good fiber; from ~7.5–8 months give boiled broccoli pieces for independent eating.
16. **Flaxseed powder** — a pinch once/twice a day → smoother potty + omega-3 for brain.
17. **Stocks** (non-veg + veg) — use the leftover boiling water for soup or khichdi.
18. **Banana** — a good laxative (smoothie, porridge, or mashed; prefer fresh over Kerala-banana powder).
19. **Papaya** — mashed, in pieces, or as a smoothie.
20. **Pumpkin / bottle gourd / red pumpkin** — easily boiled, good fiber; add to soups/smoothies/food.
21. **Lime/lemon juice** — a few drops in non-sweet food: probiotic, aids digestion, improves nutrient/vitamin-C absorption in the large intestine.
22. **Coconut water / buttermilk** — increase the baby's water intake (no milk, but curd & buttermilk are fine).
23. **Rice done right** — first cereal is rice; use rice that overcooks to a paste (easy to digest). If issues, try **brown rice soaked 7–8 hrs**. Doesn't cause constipation when cooked soft.
24. **Lentils with peel** — use moong/green-moong/masoor dal **with peel** (the 5 allowed dals) to boost fiber.
25. **Buttermilk** — a great **soluble fiber** (e.g., boil the gourd-type vegetable with peel, mash, give as a snack puree).

## Cultural / product remedies (treat medicines like medicines — doctor first; don't give nightly out of habit)
- **Carom-seed (ajwain) water** — boil 1 spoon in 1 cup water 5 min.
- **Asafoetida (hing) water** applied around the tummy — relieves gas (watch for rashes; check it suits the baby).
- **MyDvija Tummy Roll-on** — carom/fennel/asafoetida extracts to reduce cramps.
- **MyDvija Herbal Potty** (handmade) — heat (on hot iron/pan or microwave 40–60 sec) and rub to relieve colic.
- **Coconut oil** applied around the anus — reduces dryness.
- **Lukewarm filtered water** in the morning — a few spoons after waking.
- **Ghee** — 1–2 drops in lukewarm water or in food (many remedies work by **releasing gas**, which then eases potty).
- **Paan (betel leaf)** — a breastfeeding mother eating plain paan daily; or boil paan, cool, place on the baby's tummy.
- **Colicaid / gripe water** — colic medicines (doctor first; don't make it a nightly habit).

**Mindset:** a peaceful mother → a child who handles the routine peacefully.
