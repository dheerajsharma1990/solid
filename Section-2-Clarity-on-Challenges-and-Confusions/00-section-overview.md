# Section 2 — Clarity on Challenges & Confusions (Overview)

*Source videos: "What's in it — Let's get clarity on challenges & confusions" and "Q&A — Clarity is power"*

## What this section covers
This section addresses the real-world doubts and problems of the solid-food journey:
- My child **gags, chokes, doesn't eat** — what's the parent's role in making a child an independent eater (BLW / baby-led weaning)?
- Are there **out-of-the-box tricks** to make food combinations amazing?
- What if my child is **allergic** to something?
- We eat **non-veg** — how/when to introduce it?
- **When to give cheese, when milk, how much water** (give or not)?
- How to make the child a **happy independent eater**.

The instructor stresses these lessons stay useful for many days — understand them well before moving on.

## Q&A video note
Like Section 1, the Section 2 Q&A ("Clarity is power") is delivered **as on-screen slides, not narration.** At **[00:00:55]** the instructor says *"close your work, come to the screen for 5 minutes and read all these slides."* **The actual questions and answers must be read directly from the on-screen slides** in the video (from ~00:00:55 onward). The narration only frames that the solid-food journey is lifelong, but the **initial habits and taste exposure** are built in the 6–12 month window.
