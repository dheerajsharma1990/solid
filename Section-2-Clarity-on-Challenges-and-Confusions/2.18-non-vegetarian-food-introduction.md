# 2.18 — Non-Vegetarian Food Introduction: Important Tips

- **Cook it properly.** Undercooked non-veg causes digestion problems; non-veg can be **heavy to digest** for a child.
- **Introduce slowly and gently.**
- If using **non-veg stock**, make sure it has **no salt**.
- Non-veg is a **very good source of protein, omega-3 fatty acids**, and supports **weight gain** — you can give it 100%.
- **Don't give only non-veg.** Still keep the **cereal + protein + fiber** combination, whether your family is vegetarian or non-vegetarian.

## Myth-busting
There is **no truth** to "a vegetarian child will be undernourished/weak unless they eat eggs," nor that "a non-veg child is automatically healthy." **Respect your family's culture** and focus on giving **nutritionally complete food** — that's what matters.
