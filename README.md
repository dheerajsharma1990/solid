## Core rules that recur across the whole course
- **Start solids at 6 months** (5 months complete if already on formula; corrected age for premature babies). Confirm with readiness signs.
- **Every meal = cereal + protein + fiber.** Milk stays primary nutrition under 1 year; **milk must never become the child's "food."**
- **No** pureed/mashed/grinded food (teeth not needed — tongue/gums/cheeks chew); **no** force feeding, **no** distraction/screen feeding, **no** bribes/fear.
- **Before 11–12 months:** no salt, no sugar, no honey, no animal (cow/buffalo/goat) milk, no bottle by 11 months. Curd from 6 months, cheese ~8 months.
- The parent's job is to **offer** the right food at the right time; **whether and how much the child eats is the child's decision.** Patience > equipment.
