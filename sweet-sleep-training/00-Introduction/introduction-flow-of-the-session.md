# Introduction — Flow of the Session

*By Shrreya Shah (MyDvija). This is a **recording of a live sleep-training class** taught to mothers — done live (rather than to camera) so the answers map to real questions every mother asks.*

## The mindset to start with
- **Sleep training is NOT a "cry-it-out" method.** The instructor's certification is explicitly **"peaceful sleep training"** — you do **not** have to make the child cry.
- **Find your "why."** Before starting, honestly answer *why* you bought this course / want the child to sleep at night. When the "why" is clear, the actions become easy.
- A child sleeping well is **physical-development management, not emotion management** — treat it as a real need to solve, not as the child being difficult.

## Course structure (4 parts + Q&As + bonus)
- **Part 1 — Why your child isn't sleeping** (customized: why *your* child wakes 5×/night even after you keep them awake for hours). Identifies exactly what to improve.
- **Q&A** after each part — a short section answering the core questions mothers raise.
- **Part 2 — Preparation for sleep training** (the step most people skip): feed & food schedule, nap schedule, environment, gaps between naps, night bedtime, bedtime routine — built into a **customized, age-wise plan** (not generic Google/Instagram advice).
- **Part 3 — Methods + a day-wise plan** (what to do on day 1, day 2, day 3 … through day 7).
- **Part 4 — Bonus section:** sleep training with teething, travel, jet lag, illness, "master weeks"/miracle weeks (regression & growth phases) and their effect on sleep; plus 4 more bonus videos — **bedtime massage & stretches, baby relaxation/calm-down, and "nap chat."**

## How to use the course (important)
- **Don't just watch for 15–20 days.** Watch for **1–2 days max, then start implementing.**
- **Target:** within **7–10 days** the baby sleeps longer and more peacefully (and so do you). Concretely (age-dependent): baby sleeps **3–5 hours at a stretch at night**, feeds, then another **3–5 hours** — ideally building to sleeping through the night.
- A **"Material" tab** sits next to the content column — open it for **notes** on exactly what to read/know while doing the course.
- **Support options:** a **personal consultation with Shrreya** and a **"10 days of hand-holding"** program are available (team contact given at the end of the recording) if you need guidance or get scared mid-way.
