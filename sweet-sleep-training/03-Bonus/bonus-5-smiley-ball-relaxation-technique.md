# Bonus 5 — Tricks to Relax the Baby & Sleep Fast (Smiley-Ball Massage)

*A demonstration video (on a 14-day-old). A **soft smiley ball** (easily available in the market) is used for a gentle massage that **stimulates acupressure points** and relaxes the baby — anyone can do it at home; it relaxes the parent too.*

## How to do it
Roll the soft ball **very gently (no real pressure)** over the body, **5 times** at each spot, while engaging the baby:
1. **Palms** — roll over the whole palm, **5×** each (stimulates acupressure points → whole-body response).
2. **Feet/soles** — tap/roll **1–2–3–4–5** on each foot.
3. **Tummy** — gently roll, **1–5** (best done with the baby undressed since the ball is soft).
4. **Hands/arms** — **1–5** each.
5. **Back & neck** — turn the baby onto the tummy (both hands kept in front for comfort), roll **1–5** on the back, **1–5** on the neck, then **draw a "V"** (the tickle creates movement), **1–5**.

A **full-body smiley-ball massage** like this relaxes the baby and helps them fall asleep peacefully — good for babies who don't sleep well at night.
