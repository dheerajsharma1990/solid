# Bonus 2 — Bedtime Massage & Stretches for Peaceful Sleep

*A demonstration video (on a 14-day-old model baby). ~80% of new parents report poor night sleep; this 5–10-min bedtime massage helps. One follower reported her baby slept 4 hours for the first time after using these techniques.*

## Morning vs. night massage
- **Morning:** to warm up and stretch the night-stiff body and boost blood circulation.
- **Night (this video):** **short (5–10 min), calming not exciting**; strokes go **toward the heart** (reverse direction) to calm the body; **palm and sole points** (which map to internal organs) are massaged for instant relaxation.

## Setup
- **Warm surroundings** (a MyDvija herbal pot nearby helps); **cooler/fan/AC off** during the massage for coziness.
- A **mattress** (keep the same oily one for evenings) + a spare cloth aside (for any puking).
- **Very little oil** — 1–2 drops from a dropper; choose oil with **essential-oil content** (MyDvija baby oil = almond/haldi/essential/coconut). If too oily, dab with dry cotton; if the child doesn't suit oil, use shea butter/moisturizer.
- **Feed ½–¼ hour BEFORE the massage** (not after) to avoid vomiting during it; feed again after the massage to sleep.

## Sequence (talk to the baby, keep eye contact, gentle music)
1. **Whole-body glide** — oil and touch the whole body from shoulders to feet (creates coziness).
2. **Tummy — clockwise circles**, following the digestion path, **5–10×** (passes gas, aids digestion).
3. **"I Love You" strokes** on chest/tummy, bottom to top, **5–6×**.
4. **Feet** — gentle finger movements + press where toes start; **sole bottom-to-top 5×**; then whole-foot **reverse strokes toward the heart (1–10)**; then **firm "press-press-press" 5×** on each sole (like relieving tired adult feet).
5. **Hands** — same as feet: finger movements, base of fingers, palm upward 5–10×, then the same strokes on the arms.
6. **Back** — gentle oiling + "I Love You" strokes.

## Stretches (especially for colic/gas)
- **Happy Baby Pose** — fold both legs, hold the feet **out to the sides** (not on the belly), gently rock **1–10**.
- **Knees-to-tummy** — both knees joined, pressed gently to the tummy, hold **5–10 counts** (releases gas).
- **Arm cross-stretch** — arms out, then crossed in, gentle, repeat.
- **Leg cross-stretch** — cross the feet gently, repeat.

## Close
Aim for **at least one parent giving a massage** (mom morning → other parent evening). Wipe excess oil, dress the baby (full-sleeve if using a fan/cooler for cozy warmth) — the baby will yawn and be sleepy; then feed and put to sleep.
