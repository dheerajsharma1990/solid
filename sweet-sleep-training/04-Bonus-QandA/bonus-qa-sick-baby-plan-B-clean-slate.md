# Bonus Q&A — Sleep Training Disrupted by Illness ("Plan B / Clean Slate")

*(Filename says "bottle-feeding-solution," but the actual question is: "Baby was getting sleep-trained with good results, then got a **fever** — now the same techniques aren't working.")*

- For fever/travel specifics, see the bonus section. But the real fix here is **Plan B = "clean the slate."**
- **Plan B:** for **4 days, do nothing** — stop trying, just go with the flow (feed/respond whenever, however). The reason: when you keep *trying* and it keeps failing, your mind starts each attempt expecting failure — so **go blank for 4 days** to reset.
- **After 4 days, restart from the very beginning** as if doing sleep training for the **first time** — step 1, step 2, step 3 with a brand-new approach.
- It then takes ~**15 days total** (the clean-slate restart takes the full ~7 days again), but doing it with a genuinely **clean slate** is what makes it work.
